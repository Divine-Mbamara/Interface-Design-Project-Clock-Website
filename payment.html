Payment Security

Your security is our priority. All payments are processed using secure, encrypted systems to ensure your information is safe. We do not store your payment details on our website.

We utilize SSL encryption and other industry-leading security technologies to keep your personal and payment information safe.

We use your information to:

Provide, operate, and maintain our website and services

Improve, personalize, and expand our website.

Communicate with you, including sending updates and promotional materials if you opt-in.

Understand how users interact with our website to enhance user experience.

Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud.

How to Pay

Select your Items: Add the items you wish to purchase to your cart.

Proceed to Checkout: Go to the checkout page and enter your shipping details.

Choose Payment Method: Select your preferred payment method from the options provided.

Confirm Payment: Complete your order by entering your payment information securely.

Receive Confirmation: Once your payment is confirmed, you will receive an order confirmation via email.

Billing Information

For payment verification, please ensure that the billing address provided matches the address associated with your card or payment method. This helps prevent delays in processing your order.

Currency Accepted

We process payments in CAD, and the final amount will be displayed in this currency during checkout. For international orders, your bank or payment provider may charge additional fees or apply currency conversion rates.
