1. Shipping Methods & Delivery Times

We offer several shipping options to fit your needs. Delivery times may vary based on your location, shipping method, and product availability.

Standard Shipping: Estimated delivery time is 5 to 7 business days.

Expedited Shipping: Estimated delivery time is 2 to 3 business days.

Next-Day Shipping: Available on orders placed before 3 PM for next business day delivery.

International Shipping: Typically 2 to 4 weeks, depending on the destination.

Please note: Delivery times may vary during peak seasons or due to factors outside our control, such as weather or customs delays.

2. Shipping Costs

Shipping fees are calculated based on the shipping method, weight, and delivery location. Here is a breakdown of our rates:

Standard Shipping: Free on orders over $50; otherwise, the cost is $5.

Expedited Shipping: $15.

Next-Day Shipping: $25.

International Shipping: International Shipping: Varies by destination.

You will be notified of the exact shipping cost before you complete your purchase during checkout.

3. Order Processing Time

Orders are processed on business days (Monday to Friday, excluding holidays). Please allow 1 to 2 business days for processing before shipping. If you place an order on the weekend or a holiday, processing will begin the following business day.

4. Tracking Your Order

Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to track your order. You can use this information to monitor your shipment's progress.

5. Shipping Restrictions

We currently ship to the following regions:

Domestic: Ships within Canada.

International: Ships to the United States, UK, and Australia.

Please note that certain products may not be available for shipping to all regions due to local regulations or shipping constraints.

6. Order Changes & Cancellations

Once an order has been processed and shipped, we cannot change or cancel it. However, if you need to make changes before it ships, please contact us as soon as possible. We will do our best to accommodate your request.

7. Damaged or Lost Shipments

If your order arrives damaged, or you believe it has been lost in transit, please contact our customer support team within 7 days of receiving your order. We will work with the shipping carrier to resolve the issue and ensure you receive your items.

8. Delivery to PO Boxes & APO/FPO Addresses

We do offer delivery to PO Boxes and APO/FPO addresses. However, delivery times may be longer, and we recommend using a physical address whenever possible for faster shipping.

9. Signature Requirement

For high-value orders, we may require a signature upon delivery to ensure your package is safely received. You will be notified if this is the case.

10. Holiday Shipping Schedule

During the holiday season, delivery times may be extended due to high shipping volumes and weather-related delays. We recommend placing orders early to ensure timely delivery.
