Return & Refund Policy

At Timeless Inc., we aim to ensure that you are completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not entirely happy with your product, we are here to help with easy returns and refund options.

1. Returns

Eligibility: To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging.

Return Period: You have 10 days from the date you received the item to initiate a return.

Non-Returnable Items: Some items, such as perishable goods, custom products, and personal care items, are not eligible for return. Please check the product description or contact us for more information.

2. How to Return

a. Contact Us: Email us at timeless.inc@gmail.com with your order details and reason for return.

b. Shipping: Once your return is approved, you will receive a shipping label or instructions on where to send your item. Return shipping costs may be your responsibility, unless the item was defective or incorrect.

c. Processing: Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

3. Refunds

Approval: If your return is approved, we will initiate a refund to your original payment method.

Processing Time: Please allow 3 - 5 business days for the refund to be processed and applied to your account. Processing times may vary depending on your payment provider.

Partial Refunds: In certain situations, only partial refunds are granted (e.g., items not in original condition, items returned after the return period).

4. Exchanges

If you need to exchange an item for a different size, color, or another variant, please contact us. Exchanges are subject to product availability.

5. Late or Missing Refunds

If you have not received a refund yet:

Check your bank account or credit card statement.

Contact your credit card company; it may take some time before your refund is officially posted.

If you have done all of this and still have not received your refund, please contact us at timeless.inc@gmail.com.
